🇺🇸 Missouri Programs

💙 MO HealthNet for Kids (MHK)

Missouri's health insurance for children under 19. Often free or very low cost based on household income. No deductibles or co-pays for most families.

📞
Apply or Get Help
Call toll-free or apply online. Applications available in English, Spanish, Bosnian, Vietnamese.
📞 1-888-275-5908
⚖️
Legal Aid — MHK Advocacy
Free legal help if your child's MHK application was rejected or coverage was terminated.
📞 816-474-6750
👩‍🍼
MO HealthNet for Pregnant Women / Families
Also available for pregnant women and low-income parents. Contact the same number.

🌾 Kansas Programs

💙 Kansas HealthWave

Children's insurance for Kansans who don't have access to affordable health insurance. Some families qualify for no premium coverage.

📞 1-800-792-4884
📱 For New Parents

👶 Text4baby

Free weekly text messages about your pregnancy and your baby's first year of life.

Text BABY to 511411

Most programs below are free to join and require no prescription insurance. Income limits apply to some programs.

💊
Missouri RX
State prescription program for seniors and those with disabilities in Missouri.
📞 1-800-375-1406
🏥
KC Medicine Cabinet
Emergency prescription help, once per year. Household income under 200% federal poverty level. Call for appointment.
📞 Bishop Sullivan: 816-231-0984 📞 Community Assistance: 816-763-3277 📞 Community Services League: 816-254-4100
💙
Pfizer Connection to Care
No age limit. Income under $19,000/single or $31,000/family. Free or discounted Pfizer medications including Lipitor, Norvasc, Zoloft.
📞 1-866-706-2400
🤝
Together RX Access Card
Under 65 years old. Income under $30,000/single or $60,000/family. No prescription coverage required. 25–40% savings. Free card.
📞 1-800-444-4106
💉
Merck Prescription Discount
For all uninsured patients regardless of age or income. Instant savings at the pharmacy counter. Also covers adult and children's vaccinations.
📞 1-800-506-3725
🛒
ScriptSave WellRx
Not insurance — a savings card usable at 55,000+ pharmacies nationwide for brand name and generic drugs. Free to use.
🌐 wellrx.com
